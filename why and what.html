Why this Kolaveri di webpage?

In 2022, I struggled more than A. Pandey while trying to find out the merit lists. All I could do was to take screenshots of YouTube videos and make my phone a mess. So, I thought that it would be nice to have a centralised database of all the merit lists.

What can you do?

You can do Anything. But fix my grammatical mistakes first. If I have missed some of the merit lists than you can MAIL ME THE LISTS or fork this repo on GitHub and create a pull request.

What more can be done?

We could leave the Christmas lights up till January and add some details about the neighbourhood of the colleges and provide information about the availability of rooms/PGs and other stuffs so that the new students get at least an idea about the Visages, Villages (Faces Places).

This is our place, we make the rules. You can share more ideas to make this database better.
